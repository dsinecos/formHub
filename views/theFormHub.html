FormHub

Collect your form submissions on your email 

                        
    < form method="POST" 
    action="https://api-formhub.herokuapp.com/v1/youremail@mail.com">

        < input type="text" name="firstName">
        < input type="text" name="lastName">
        < input type="text" name="address">
        < input type="submit">
    
    </form>
  1. Setup the action attribute in your form to "https://api-formhub.herokuapp.com/v1/your@mail.com"

  2. Provide a name attribute to the input fields

  3. You're ready to receive your form submissions on the email you provided your@email.com
